Money & What's Covered

What's Included

Short version

Everything is covered.

The group covers the entire trip — including all meals. You only need money for personal extras.

What the group covers

  • ASCEND event registration
  • Hotel stay — La Quinta Lynnwood, Saturday night
  • Transportation & gas
  • All meals — Saturday lunch, Saturday dinner, Sunday breakfast, Sunday lunch

$ What to bring money for

  • Personal extras — gas station snacks, coffee upgrades, anything outside the group meals
  • Shopping — ASCEND merchandise, souvenirs, mall stores, etc.
  • Offerings — a few dollars for the collection basket at Latin Mass and St. James
  • Suggested amount — $20–40 for personal spending, depending on how much extra you want
Payment status

Paid in full by the group.

Nothing is owed by attendees. If you have any questions, contact Gaby — +1 (509) 823-9987 or gaby@sjdyoungadults.com.

FAQ

"What if I want to buy merch at ASCEND?"

Bring your own money. You can browse the ASCEND merch store ahead of time to plan: ASCEND Merch Store →

"Are all meals really covered?"

Yes — Saturday lunch in Bellevue, Saturday dinner near the hotel, Sunday breakfast, and Sunday lunch are all covered by the group. If you want something extra on top of that (like a gas station snack run or a coffee stop), that's on you.

"What if I run out of cash?"

Most Bellevue and Seattle restaurants take cards. Meydenbauer's merch tables typically take cards too. There will be ATMs nearby if you need one.

"What if I can't afford the trip?"

Please talk to one of the group leaders before the event so we can figure something out. We would absolutely not want money to be the reason you don't come — we'll get you covered. Just reach out to us. No one gets left behind.
