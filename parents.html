What your young adult is doing

Saturday: Drive from Tieton to Bellevue for a Catholic conference called ASCEND. They'll hear talks from nationally known Catholic speakers (Chris Stefanick, Dr. Tim Gray, the Swaffords), attend Mass celebrated by Archbishop Paul Etienne, and join a Eucharistic missionary commissioning. After ASCEND, the group drives to Lynnwood and stays overnight at the La Quinta Inn.

Sunday: Attend the 7:00 AM Traditional Latin Mass at North American Martyrs Parish in Edmonds, then breakfast. Visit St. James Cathedral in downtown Seattle for prayer, fellowship, lunch, and then the drive back to Yakima — home by about 7:00–7:30 PM.

Safety & supervision

How we're taking care of your young adult.

  • Chaperoned by Gaby, Alex, and Johana throughout the trip
  • Guys and girls in separate hotel rooms — no exceptions
  • 11:00 PM curfew Saturday night (Mass is early Sunday)
  • The group travels together — no one is left alone
  • All chaperones have phones and can be reached anytime
  • Emergency contact is available 24/7 during the trip

Cost

Fully covered by the group. Your young adult only needs pocket money for snacks and any personal purchases (ASCEND merch, souvenirs, offerings).
