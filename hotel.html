Why this hotel

  • Recently renovated — very clean, well maintained
  • Free hot breakfast available (note: we leave at 6:15 AM Sunday, before breakfast service starts — we eat after Mass instead)
  • Free parking on site
  • Indoor pool, hot tub, and gym
  • Close to I-5 and Lynnwood light rail
  • Reviewed for friendly staff and clean rooms

Strategically located

Between all three of our destinations:

  • ~20 min to Meydenbauer Center (Bellevue)
  • ~15 min to North American Martyrs (Edmonds)
  • ~20 min to St. James Cathedral (Seattle)
Chosen because it's affordable, comfortable, and right in the center of our weekend loop — less drive time means more rest and more retreat.
