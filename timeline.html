Why these times?

The drive from Tieton to Bellevue is 142 miles (~2h 25m clean). We pad it with: 15 min group loading · 15 min Snoqualmie Pass slowdown buffer · 15 min mid-drive rest stop · 20 min Seattle-area traffic · 20 min parking, walking, and check-in.

Total realistic door-to-seat: about 2 hours, 45 minutes. Option A (With Confession & Adoration) arrives 30 min before 7 AM Adoration. Option B (Without Confession & Adoration) arrives 45 min before the 9 AM opening. Both have buffer for bad conditions.
