~145 mi · ~2h 30m clean · Summitview Rd → I-82 W → I-90 W → I-405 N

La Quinta → North American Martyrs

04

NAM → La Quinta → Kona Kitchen → Gas Works Park

~15 min NAM → hotel · ~5 min hotel → Kona Kitchen · ~25–30 min Lynnwood → Gas Works Park

Sunday morning loop: head back to the hotel after Mass to check out and change, breakfast at Kona Kitchen Lynnwood (3805 196th St SW), then south on I-5 to Gas Works for skyline views.

Open
05

Gas Works → St. James Cathedral

~10–15 min · I-5 S

Mother church of the Archdiocese of Seattle. Visit, prayer, light candles.

Open
06

St. James → Pike Place + Waterfront

~5–10 min to Pike Place garage · 3+ hours of free time

Park at the Pike Place Market PDA Garage (1531 Western Ave) — direct elevator to MarketFront and the new Overlook Walk down to the waterfront. Split into small groups, eat lunch on your own at any spot near Pike Place, meet at Pier 62 for the group photo at ~3:45 PM.

Open
07

Pike Place → Cowiche (St. Juan Diego)

~145 mi · ~2h 30m · I-5 S → I-90 E → I-82 E → Summitview Rd

Depart the Pike Place garage ~4:15 PM after the group photo. Drop-off back at St. Juan Diego ~6:45 PM, give or take traffic over Snoqualmie Pass.

Open
⚠

Check Snoqualmie Pass the night before

Even in May, pass conditions can slow you down. If there's snow or heavy rain, add buffer time and wake up a little earlier.

Parking

Meydenbauer Center

434 underground parking spaces. Arrive early so the group parks together.

Directions & parking →
ASCEND venue info →

La Quinta Lynnwood

Free parking on site. Park near the lobby entrance for check-in.

North American Martyrs Parish

Free parish lot. It can fill up for Sunday Mass — arrive 15 minutes early.

Gas Works Park

Free parking lot at the park. Can fill up on sunny weekends — arrive early or park on nearby streets.

St. James Cathedral

Street parking on Sundays is typically available. Metered spots downtown Seattle — bring quarters or pay via app.

Pike Place Market PDA Garage

1531 Western Ave, Seattle, WA 98101. 800 spaces, open 5 AM–2 AM. The safest, most direct option for the waterfront — elevator straight up to MarketFront and the new Overlook Walk down to Pier 62. Sunday rates: roughly $8 first hour, $25–$40 for a 3–4 hour stay (we are there ~3h 45m).
